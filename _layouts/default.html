American Film Institute

100 Years...100 Movies

"A 'definitive selection of the 100 greatest American movies of all time, as determined by more than 1,500 leaders from the American film community.' The 400 Nominated Films (original) were feature-length fictional movies produced between 1912 and 1996 'with the goal of amassing a capsule of the first 100 years of American cinema, across decades and across genres.'"

Two versions of the list exist: the original 1998 selection and another a 10th Anniversary selection in 2007 (added eligibility from 1997 to 2005).
